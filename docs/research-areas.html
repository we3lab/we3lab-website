Research

The Water & Energy Efficiency for the Environment Lab (WE3Lab) develops and applies process-based and systems-level optimization models to improve the affordability, resiliency, and sustainability of water-energy infrastructure systems. Our computational tools guide robust infrastructure planning, prioritize technology innovation and investment, and unlock system flexibility.
