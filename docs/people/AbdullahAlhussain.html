Abdullah Alhussain

MS Student

Abdullah is a Master’s student in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University. He earned his Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of California, Irvine, where he served as an energy consultant through the U.S. Department of Energy’s Industrial Assessment Center, conducting energy efficiency audits for manufacturing facilities. He is currently involved in customer discovery for WaterTAP, exploring market applications and user needs for water treatment and sustainability solutions.
